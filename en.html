About us

«The Well» is that place to dive into the rich history of Prussia.

We expose a one-of-a-kind collection of historical artifacts from the once-thriving city, long lost to the ravages of war..

The documentary crafted by our team, showcases historical evidence and interviews with the founders of this agglomeration. Paired with the captivating light spectacle, it reveals fascinating unknown facts that will leave you amazed.

The Ponarth

Our museum is situated in the heart of the old Ponarth, factory, which held great importance in the history of Koenigsberg.

It serves as a testament to the poverful manufacturing center and holds the secrets of an intricate network of underground passages, that interconnect the city.

The Well Spectacle

Colorful light show amidst the waterlogged yet funcitional well, made possible by a sophisticated combination of glass structure, along with lighting and draining systems allows you to take a glimpse into the depths.

Feel the world of stunning Prussian tales and legends through stunning projections accompanied by musical compositions.

The Well Spectacle brings history to life and creates unforgettable experiences.
